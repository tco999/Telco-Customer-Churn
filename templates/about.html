What is Churn?

Churn is when existing customers stop doing business with you. This can mean different things depending on the nature of your business. For companies that operating on a subscription-based model, we need to calculate Churn rate. According to Investopedia.com, Churn rate, also known as the rate of attrition or customer churn, is the rate at which customers stop doing business with an entity. It is most commonly expressed as the percentage of service subscribers who discontinue their subscriptions within a given time period. Churn is a growth decelerator, a silent but deadly if left unseen.
